

## 🎤 AI Sharing Session: Rules & Flow

### Organizer Wrap-up

* To close out the session, the organizer will present a **10-minute update** covering last week's progress and the upcoming plan for Agent Development.
1. Best way to learn: teaching other guys. reffer to **Feynman Technique**, so i organize this group for you and me.

### 1. Presentation Time & Sequence

* **Standard Time:** Each speaker has **3 minutes**.
* **Over Adjustment:** If the total group people exceeds 10, individual time drops to **3 minutes** to keep the pace brisk.
* **Order of Speakers:** Everyone will draw a poker card to determine their speaking order, along with receiving voting tokens.

### 2. The Pitch (What to Share)

* Briefly share a real-life or workplace case where you used AI.
* Focus purely on a **simple, quick walkthrough** of the workflow.
* 3 sentences:
* 1> **which** question? 2> **how** to use AI to improve it? 3> **show** me the flow(laptop or whiteboard)

### 3. Voting & The Deep Dive

* **Token Voting:** After each presentation, you can give your tokens to the speakers you want to hear more from.
* **Top 3 Finalists:** The top 3 voted speakers will get extra time at the whiteboard to dive deep into their workflows.
* *Note: It is highly recommended to bring your laptop to easily show the step-by-step details.*
* Everyone who got any tokens, can leave one **question** to the group to improe his/her real question.
